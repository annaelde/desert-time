ADAM

Released on January 10, 2018 for Windows, Linux, and Mac.

ADAM is a story about an android's search for meaning. Experience the world of CITY-12, a dystopian future, through the eyes of ADAM, a sentient android. Journey with him from the factory he was born in and into the world beyond.

  • Explore CITY-12, a feature-filled open world.
  • Complete quests to aid ADAM's search for meaning.
  • Fight advanced AI with ADAM's unique android abilities.
