About Us

Desert Time Studios was founded in Orlando, Florida in 2016 by a small team of indie game developers. We consider game design to be an art-form, just like music, movies, and literature, so our team aims to create games that capture the imagination and provide tough dilemmas for the player to explore. We've released three games so far across desktop and mobile platforms.
